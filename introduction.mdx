# Welcome to ServeStream Documentation

ServeStream is a powerful platform designed to optimize restaurant management and customer service through innovative AI technologies. It provides two primary solutions:

1. **Restaurant Management**: A comprehensive solution that integrates with POS Platforms for seamless menu synchronization and efficient restaurant workflows.
2. **AI Voice Scheduling Agent**: An AI-powered system that manages customer calls, handling both reservations and takeout orders with ease.

This documentation will guide you through the setup and usage of both solutions, with step-by-step instructions for integrating Square, Clover, syncing menus, and using the AI voice system for managing customer interactions.

By the end of this guide, you will be equipped to streamline your restaurant operations and enhance the customer experience with AI-driven features.
